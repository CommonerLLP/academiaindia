About this project

An independent, public-interest tracker of faculty positions in India's centrally-funded higher-education institutions, paired with a corpus-driven investigation into how the constitutional reservation regime is being administered in faculty hiring. Built on 741 parliamentary questions, multi-year vacancy disclosures, and an ongoing scrape of institutional advertisements.

Listings are scraped from official institutional websites and parsed automatically. Mistakes are possible — extraction errors, stale data, missing posts. Always verify against the official advertisement and application portal before applying. This site does not replace the original notification; it surfaces it.

The Constitution is not being violated by accident. It is being administered to that effect.

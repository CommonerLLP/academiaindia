About this project

An independent, public-interest tracker of academic work in India — faculty, visiting, and postdoctoral positions across central HEIs, IIMs and IITs, AIIMS, and major private universities. Paired with a corpus-driven investigation, in The Gap, into how the state's vacancy-disclosure regime is being administered. Built on 741 parliamentary questions, multi-year vacancy disclosures, and an ongoing scrape of institutional advertisements.

No warranty; verify before applying. Listings are scraped from official institutional websites, parsed automatically, and occasionally hand-transcribed from circulated recruitment cards. Errors are possible — extraction errors, stale data, missing posts, transcription gaps. Always verify against the official advertisement and application portal before applying. This site does not replace the original notification; it surfaces it.

Independence and use at own risk. The site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated on behalf of any institution listed. The maintainer makes no warranty as to the accuracy, completeness, currency, or availability of any position. Use of this information is at the reader's own risk. The site collects no personal data about visitors; it stores no application records and acts as an index, not an intermediary.

Analytical content. Material in The Gap is the maintainer's research interpretation of publicly available parliamentary records, court orders, and official Ministry communications, all of which are cited in the source bibliography on that page. It is offered as research-and-reference public-interest commentary; it is not legal advice and not an allegation against any individual.

The Constitution is not being violated by accident. It is being administered to that effect.
